Sourdough Bread

Unbleached white flour:	650 grams
Whole-wheat flour:		75 grams
Water:				420 grams
Levain:				200 grams
Salt:				14 grams

Make leaven the night before with:

Sourdough starter:		50 grams
Unbleached flour:		66 grams
Water:				84 grams

Mix all the ingredients together to form dough.  Knead on a surface that is
un-floured, until the dough is no longer sticky. Put back into bowl and cover
with plastic wrap or tea towel.  Let rise in  warm place for 2-3 hours, folding
the dough every hour.
